Wall Mount FAQ

Do wall mounts damage the wall?

Short answer: Adhesive mounts are renter‑friendly.

Most gaming wall mounts use adhesive or small screws. Adhesive mounts leave minimal marks.

Can wall mounts hold heavy headsets?

Short answer: Yes — most support 2–5 lbs.

Perfect for gaming headsets, controllers, and small accessories.

Are RGB wall panels worth it?

Short answer: Yes — they dramatically improve aesthetics.

RGB panels add depth, color, and ambiance to your setup and look amazing on camera.
