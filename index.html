Gaming Setup FAQ

What do I need for a clean gaming setup?

A good desk, cable management, RGB lighting, and a monitor mount make the biggest difference. A good desk gives you a solid, clutter-free foundation. Cable management hides messy wires to keep the look sleek. RGB lighting adds a personalized atmosphere and backlighting. Monitor mounts free up valuable desk space under your screen.

What RGB lights are best?

LED strips for glow, wall panels for aesthetics, and neon signs for personality. LED strips best for ambient backlighting behind your desk or monitor. Wall panels are best for modular, geometric art designs on the wall. Neon signs are best for adding a pop of unique, personalized style.
