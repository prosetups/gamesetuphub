Gaming Desk FAQ

What size desk is best for gaming?

Short answer: 48–60 inches fits most setups.

Ultra‑wide or dual‑monitor setups benefit from 60–72 inches.

Are RGB gaming desks worth it?

Short answer: Yes — if you want aesthetics + features.

They often include cable management, cup holders, and headset hooks.

Is an L‑shaped desk better for gaming?

Short answer: Yes — for multi‑monitor setups.

L‑shaped desks maximize corner space and provide more surface area.
