Peripherals FAQ

Are mechanical keyboards better for gaming?

Short answer: Yes — they offer faster response times and better durability.

Mechanical switches provide tactile feedback and customizable performance.

What mouse DPI is best for gaming?

Short answer: 800–1600 DPI for most gamers.

Higher DPI is useful for large monitors or fast‑paced FPS games.

Do RGB peripherals improve performance?

Short answer: No — RGB is purely aesthetic.

But it improves visibility and enhances your setup’s look.
