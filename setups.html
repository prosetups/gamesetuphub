Gaming Setup FAQ

How do I build a clean gaming setup?

Short answer: Use cable management + minimal accessories.

Start with a clean desk, route cables behind the desk, and use RGB lighting for ambiance.

What’s the best lighting for gaming setups?

Short answer: LED strips + RGB wall panels.

They create depth, color, and a modern aesthetic.

How much does a good gaming setup cost?

Short answer: $300–$2,000 depending on your goals.

Budget setups start low, while RGB setups cost more due to lighting and accessories.
