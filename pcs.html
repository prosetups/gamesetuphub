PC Build FAQ

Is it cheaper to build or buy a gaming PC?

Short answer: Building is usually cheaper.

Prebuilts are more convenient, but custom builds offer better performance per dollar.

What PC build is best for 1440p gaming?

Short answer: RTX 4070 or RX 7800 XT builds.

These GPUs offer the best balance of performance and value for 1440p.

Do RGB PC builds run hotter?

Short answer: No — RGB has almost no thermal impact.

Airflow and case design matter far more than lighting.
